Templates are pages that are embedded (transcluded) into other pages to allow for the repetition of information.

  • Help:A quick guide to templates, a brief introduction on templates for beginners.
  • Help:Template, the main technical help page on templates, provides information on creating & using templates.
  • Wikipedia:Template namespace,guidelines and tips for use of templates.
  • Wikipedia:WikiProject Templates, the WikiProject that looks after template-related issues.
  • Wikipedia:Template index, an index of all standard templates used on Wikipedia, grouped into topic-specific.
    • Wikipedia:Navigation templates,templates that link between multiple articles belonging to the same topic.
    • Wikipedia:List of infoboxes for infoboxes, which are small panels that summarize key features.
    • Wikipedia:Navigation templates,templates that link between multiple articles belonging to the same topic.
  • Wikipedia:Requested templates, to request creation of a template.
  • Category:Wikipedia templates.
  • Special:ExpandTemplates,expands all templates recursively.
